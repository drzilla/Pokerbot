## Ace-High Dry  [ace_high_dry]
   Example: A-7-2 rainbow
   Maps to existing parser bucket: dry_ahigh
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     40+ BB: freq=95-100%, sizing=B25
        Range bet 25% at 100BB depth
     25-40BB: freq=95-100%, sizing=B20
        Range bet 20% at shallower depth
     0-25BB (DUAL): freq=95-100%, sizing=B20/B75
        At ~24BB switch to dual: B20 with whole range, B75 heavier on Ax-in-hand
        to build pot

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=30-50%, sizing=B25/B33
        Range & nuts adv but doesn't require protection — high check freq, low bet
        sizing (B25/B33). Bet mainly with pure bluffs or hands that want to build
        a pot.

## Ace-High Coordinated  [ace_high_coordinated]
   Example: A-J-9 two-tone
   Maps to existing parser bucket: dynamic
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     40+ BB (DUAL): freq=75-85%, sizing=B50/B100
        100BB: ~80% freq dual sizing. B100 with strong Ax/sets/weak made
        (protection); B50 with weak Ax + non-Ax with BDFD
     0-40BB (DUAL): freq=90-99%, sizing=B30/B50
        Shallower: freq up to ~95%, sizing shifts down to B30/B50 split

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB (DUAL): freq=50-70%, sizing=B40/B100
        Range & nuts adv but very dynamic so requires protection — heavy on bet
        AND check-raise vs check-call. Two buckets: (1) bluffs + monsters + strong
        draws; (2) marginal made hands + weak draws + strong made hands. Bet B40,
        CR B100. Need to charge for many draws hence sizing is big.

## Broadway Disconnected  [broadway_disconnected]
   Example: K-Q-4 rainbow
   Maps to existing parser bucket: dynamic
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     40+ BB (DUAL): freq=no freq target, sizing=B33/B85/B100
        UTG vs BB pattern: bluffs + semi-bluffs + super-strong (two pair+) go big
        B85-B100; everything else B33. Polarized.

   OOP C-BET:
     40+ BB (DUAL): freq=no freq target, sizing=B33/B100
        SB vs BB pattern: low overall freq (neither has range adv). Bet big with
        strong TP+ or pure air (nuts adv only). B33 with ~1/3 of hands — top-card
        connectors (non-ultra-strong) + some sets.

   📝 3BP note: 3-bet pots are smaller c-bets vs the SRP B33/B85/B100 polarized pattern. From Dave 2026-05-13.

## Broadway Coordinated  [broadway_coordinated]
   Example: Q-J-T
   Maps to existing parser bucket: dynamic
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=95-100%, sizing=B33/B66
        Range adv → 100% c-bet. No real bluffs available → single sizing 33-66%.
        Optional dual with pure bluffs (gutters like A2 with gutter) + monsters
        (sets, flopped nuts) if splitting.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB (DUAL): freq=40-60%, sizing=B33/B66
        Range & nuts adv on a dynamic board. Two-sizing B33 + B66. B33 with
        marginal made hands and some nuts mixes; B66 with stronger value. On a
        hyper-dynamic board there's no real CR strategy — higher freq blocker
        c-bet or check-call to not bloat the pot.

## Middling Connected  [middling_connected]
   Example: 9-8-7
   Maps to existing parser bucket: connected_mid
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=75-85%, sizing=B50
        No range adv, no nuts adv, equity shifts easily. NOT low-freq — GTO c-bets
        ~80% with single sizing ~50%. Bet flopped monsters + bluffs that don't
        mind getting CR-folded (gutters). Check marginal made hands and complete
        rubbish (give up).

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=40-60%, sizing=B25
        Nuts adv-ish, range disadv, dynamic with equity shifts → lowish freq cbets
        (~50%) with small bets (B25) using basically the whole range except
        complete give-ups. In reality population over-stabs so it's better to bet
        LESS than GTO recommends — closer to 25% freq is the exploit.

## Middling Disconnected  [middling_disconnected]
   Example: 9-5-2 rainbow
   Maps to existing parser bucket: other
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     60+ BB: freq=80-95%, sizing=B100/B125/B150
        Range AND nuts adv. High freq big bet (100-150% pot) with overcards (top
        two ranks above board) and made hands. Check marginal middling made hands
        and pure-SDV hands like AKo/AQo/AJo/ATo that just want to run to showdown.
     0-60BB: freq=60-80%, sizing=B75/B100
        Shallower: check more often, smaller sizes.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=20-30%, sizing=B85
        Nuts adv-ish, range meaningless, static board (no protection needed). B85
        with ~25% freq. Range: decent made hands + draws + overcards like Qx/Jx.

## Low Connected  [low_connected]
   Example: 6-5-4
   Maps to existing parser bucket: low_dry
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=0-10%, sizing=B50/B66
        Zero range adv, no nuts adv. ~95% check. Only bet when can sustain a CR /
        need protection / don't care about CR. Checkable ~66% of the time easy.
        Note: villain (BB) should LEAD ~50% of the time on these textures.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=25-40%, sizing=B25
        Similar pattern to 9-5-2: low freq, low bets (B25). Range & nuts adv
        depend on opening position (SB-vs-BB OOP context). Same principles: made
        hands + draws + Qx/Jx. Given equity shifts and range density for villain,
        it's not big bets.

## Low Ragged  [low_ragged]
   Example: 7-3-2 rainbow
   Maps to existing parser bucket: low_dry
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     60+ BB (DUAL): freq=90-100%, sizing=B50/B100
        Massive range adv, no nuts adv → high freq, low base bet. ~95% bet with
        dual sizing B50 + B100. B100 reserved for strong made hands, strong draws,
        and some random draws. Bluffs are 'fold-equivalent' Ax/Kx with two
        overcards (Q-max).
     30-60BB (DUAL): freq=70-90%, sizing=B50/B100
        Shallower: more selective. ~40BB: bet B100 ~40% of the time.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=20-30%, sizing=B85
        OOP plays the same as middling_disconnected: nuts adv-ish, static board,
        B85 with ~25% freq using decent made hands + draws + overcards like Qx/Jx.

## Monotone  [monotone]
   Example: J-6-2 monotone
   Maps to existing parser bucket: monotone
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-04

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=85-95%, sizing=B25/B50
        Very high freq (~90%): any flush draw, any made hand, any hand that wants
        to fold weak made hands. Only check shitty made hands (e.g., A2 on AJ8m).
        Sizing 25-50% sized to fold floaters — the goal is to charge naked
        one-card flush draws and fold air.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=20-30%, sizing=B25
        Nuts adv but not range density adv, very dynamic. Low freq (~25%) with low
        bet (B25). Some hands that don't want to check-call go in infrequently —
        mix of made hands or strong draws.

## High-Low-Low Two-Tone  [high_low_low_two_tone]
   Example: K-6-2 two-tone
   Maps to existing parser bucket: other
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-06

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=80-95%, sizing=B25/B33
        Nuts & range adv → high freq, one size (B25 or B33). Can have a check
        range and becoming shallow not a must — should be marginal made hands like
        low pair, or high pair, or Ax-strong that doesn't want to face CR but
        isn't benefiting from betting.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=25-40%, sizing=B33
        Heavy check frequency — equal ranges + lots of turn equity shifts. Bet
        only strong made hands or strong pairs needing protection. Sizing 1/3 pot
        when betting. Cross-ref Hand 66 from 2026-05-06 session.

## Low Two-Tone  [low_two_tone]
   Example: 5-3-2 two-tone
   Maps to existing parser bucket: low_dry
   Status: ✅ COMPLETE (IP + OOP)
   Source: Dave session 2026-05-13

   IP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=0-10%, sizing=B25
        Nuts/range adv on paper, but MASSIVE potential equity shift. Only ~5% freq
        with B25. Equity is essentially identical and villain's range is more
        condensed than on a low_ragged board — the two-tone + connection adds
        backdoor straight and FD potential that destroys range adv. Sibling to
        low_ragged but more dynamic.

   OOP C-BET:
     0+ BB: freq=20-30%, sizing=B100
        Low freq, polarized. B100 — when you bet, you bet big
        (overpair-for-protection or strong nut-blocker bluff).

   📝 Note: Sibling to low_ragged but two-tone with backdoor straight potential. Classifier should distinguish 5-3-2 two-tone from 7-3-2 rainbow.
