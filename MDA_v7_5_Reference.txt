USAGE PRINCIPLE
---------------
MDA recommendations are POPULATION EXPLOITS, not GTO baselines.
They identify spots where the field's average action is structurally
−EV; deviating in the indicated direction captures the EV the field
leaves on the table. They DO NOT replace the underlying GTO range —
they represent the SLICE of the GTO range that captures most of the
+EV at that node.

When an MDA recommendation conflicts with Dave J-series (e.g. an MDA
3-bet sizing differs from J44), Dave wins. When MDA conflicts with
Ron's confirmed session leak (e.g. a recurring leak says Ron is
already over-frequent here), Ron's data wins.

RECOMMENDATION 1 — BvB BB iso-raise vs SB limp (30+BB stacks)
==============================================================
TRIGGER:
  Position: BB
  Opener: SB (limp)
  Stack: 30+BB effective
RECOMMENDED RANGE (~9% of hands, value-heavy):
  66+, A7s+, A9o+, J9s+, J9o+
  Expanded: 66, 77, 88, 99, TT, JJ, QQ, KK, AA,
            A7s, A8s, A9s, ATs, AJs, AQs, AKs,
            A9o, ATo, AJo, AQo, AKo,
            J9s, JTs, J9o, JTo
SIZING: 3.5–5x BB (e.g., raise to 4 BB when BB is 1 BB).
EV (per in-range raise): +3.1 BB
  - SB folds 41.6% × +1.0 BB = +0.42 BB
  - SB calls (showdown win) 48.6% × +6.3 BB = +3.06 BB
  - SB raises (3-bet) 5.9% × −2.5 BB = −0.15 BB
  - SB jams ~28-bb 3.9% × −5.0 BB = −0.20 BB
SAMPLE: 14,978 events, 95% CI [+3.34, +9.24] for showdowns. HIGH confidence.
BACKGROUND: Population raises 30% vs SB limps; only ~9% are individually +EV.
            The wider-than-9% portion is neutral or −EV because SB calls too light.

INTERACTION WITH DAVE J29:
  J29 says BvB SB pot-entry is ~80% limp at deep stacks. This
  recommendation is the BB SIDE of the same node — it tells Hero
  what to do AS BB when SB DOES limp. They're complementary, not
  contradictory: J29 governs SB's preflop action; MDA-1 governs
  BB's iso-response.

RECOMMENDATION 2 — BB 3-bet defense vs MP open (100bb)
==============================================================
TRIGGER:
  Position: BB
  Opener: MP
  Stack: 100BB effective
  Open size: ~2.5x
RECOMMENDED 3-BET RANGE (~5%):
  99+, AQs+, AQo+
  Class threshold: 99+, AKs+, AKo+
EV (per in-range 3-bet vs MP 2.5x): +1.6 to +2.7 BB
  - MP folds (~+3 BB pot) 55% = +1.65 BB
  - MP calls (~+1.5 BB edge) 35% = +0.53 BB
  - MP 4-bets (~−6 BB) 10% = −0.60 BB
BACKGROUND: Population 3-bets only 6.6%; threshold range is +2.69 BB
            per 3-bet — significant EV left by under-defending.

COUNTER-RECOMMENDATION:
  vs SB BvB open (NOT MP), TIGHTEN 3-bet to JJ+, AKs+, AKo (~2.5%).
  Population avg vs SB-open is −3.74 BB — population over-3-bets vs SB.

RECOMMENDATION 3 — PUSH 10BB at MP (Middle Position)
==============================================================
TRIGGER:
  Position: MP
  Stack: ~10BB effective
  Action: First-in
RECOMMENDED PUSH RANGE (~34%, double the population frequency of 16.3%):
  22+, A2s+, A2o+, K5s+, K7o+, Q8s+, QTo+, JTs+, JTo+, 65s+, 97s+
EV (per jam, MP at 10BB):
  - All fold 48.8% × +3 BB dead money = +1.47 BB
  - Called 51.2% × +1.16 BB per-combo avg = +0.59 BB
  - TOTAL ≈ +1.7 BB per jam
SAMPLE: n=36,224 MP RFI events; 8,640 showdowns. HIGH confidence.

INTERACTION WITH B32 (chart corruption):
  This recommendation directly addresses the OCR-corrupted
  PUSH_10BB_MP / PUSH_10BB_HJ charts that have anchor gaps. Where
  the chart is corrupted AND a hand falls in this MDA range, treat
  this MDA range as authoritative.

RECOMMENDATION 4 — REJAM: Add Premium Pairs at 12-25BB
==============================================================
TRIGGER:
  Hero has opened OR is facing a 3-bet
  Stack: 12-25BB effective
  Spot: REJAM cells across all opener positions
ADJUSTMENT:
  Add TT+, JJ+, QQ+, KK+, AA to all REJAM cells.
  (Standard charts often omit premiums from rejams in favor of flats —
   data shows rejamming is massively +EV at this depth.)
EV (BTN vs HJ open, 15-25BB):
  - Villain folds 41.9% × +4.51 BB = +1.89 BB
  - Villain calls 58.1% × +12.20 BB = +7.09 BB
  - TOTAL ≈ +9.0 BB per REJAM
SAMPLE: n=3,718 events, 95% CI [+7.21, +10.75]. HIGH confidence.
NOTE: This is the largest single exploit in the MDA dataset.

INTERACTION WITH J33-J40 (Dave short-stack rules):
  Dave's short-stack framework already includes premium rejams at
  this depth — this MDA quantifies the EV but doesn't change Dave's
  prescription. Use this to UPGRADE confidence on borderline
  premium-rejam decisions, not to override Dave on sizing/timing.

HOW THE OVERLAY APPEARS IN THE REPORT

when ANY deviation in the section has a match. Format:
  `MDA-1: +3.1 BB if BvB iso (HIGH)`
  `MDA-3: push wider, +1.7 BB (HIGH)`

A new XIII.7 sub-section "MDA Population Exploits Triggered" lists
every hand where Hero's actual action is ALIGNED with an MDA
recommendation (positive cases) and every hand where Hero DEVIATED
FROM MDA in the wrong direction (missed exploits).
